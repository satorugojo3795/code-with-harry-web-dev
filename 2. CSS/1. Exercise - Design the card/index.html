Udaipur - the city of lakes

Udaipur (Hindi: IPA: [ʊdəjpʊɾ], pronunciationⓘ) (ISO 15919: Udayapura) is a city and municipal corporation in Udaipur district of the state of Rajasthan, India.[3][4] It is also known as the 'City of Lakes' [5] and serves as the administrative headquarters of Udaipur district.
